# Lasagna Bolognese

## Summary
Lasagna Bolognese is the ultimate Italian comfort food, featuring layers of rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, pasta sheets, and cheese. This authentic recipe from Bologna is perfect for feeding a crowd and tastes even better the next day.

## Ingredients
**For the Bolognese sauce:**
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1/2 lb ground pork
- 4 oz pancetta, finely chopped
- 1 onion, finely diced
- 2 carrots, finely diced
- 2 celery stalks, finely diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 cup red wine
- 28 oz can crushed tomatoes
- 2 tablespoons tomato paste
- 1 cup whole milk
- 1 bay leaf
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 3 tablespoons olive oil

**For the béchamel:**
- 4 tablespoons butter
- 1/4 cup flour
- 4 cups whole milk, warmed
- Pinch of nutmeg
- Salt and white pepper

**For assembly:**
- 1 lb fresh lasagna sheets (or no-boil)
- 2 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
- 1 cup Parmigiano-Reggiano, grated

## Preparation
1. For Bolognese: Heat olive oil in a large pot. Cook pancetta until crispy. Add ground beef and pork, brown well.
2. Add onion, carrots, and celery. Cook until softened, about 10 minutes. Add garlic, cook 1 minute.
3. Pour in wine, simmer until evaporated. Add tomatoes, tomato paste, bay leaf, salt, and pepper.
4. Simmer on low heat for 2 hours, stirring occasionally. Add milk in the last 30 minutes. Remove bay leaf.
5. For béchamel: Melt butter in a saucepan. Whisk in flour, cook for 2 minutes.
6. Gradually add warm milk, whisking constantly. Cook until thickened, about 10 minutes. Season with nutmeg, salt, and pepper.
7. Preheat oven to 375°F. Spread a thin layer of Bolognese in a 9x13 baking dish.
8. Layer: pasta sheets, Bolognese, béchamel, mozzarella, Parmigiano. Repeat 3-4 times.
9. Top final layer with extra béchamel and cheese.
10. Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes. Remove foil, bake 15 more minutes until golden and bubbling.
11. Let rest for 15 minutes before serving.
