# Ratatouille

## Summary
Ratatouille is a vibrant Provençal vegetable stew that celebrates the flavors of summer. This healthy and colorful dish combines eggplant, zucchini, peppers, and tomatoes with herbs, making it perfect as a side dish or light main course.

## Ingredients
- 1 large eggplant, diced into 1-inch cubes
- 2 zucchini, diced into 1-inch cubes
- 1 red bell pepper, diced
- 1 yellow bell pepper, diced
- 4 large tomatoes, diced
- 1 large onion, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 2 tablespoons tomato paste
- 1 tablespoon fresh thyme leaves
- 2 tablespoons fresh basil, chopped
- 1 bay leaf
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Fresh parsley for garnish

## Preparation
1. Salt eggplant cubes and let drain in a colander for 30 minutes. Rinse and pat dry.
2. Heat 2 tablespoons olive oil in a large pan over medium-high heat. Cook eggplant until golden, about 8 minutes. Remove and set aside.
3. Add zucchini to the pan with a little more oil. Cook until lightly browned, about 5 minutes. Remove and set aside.
4. In the same pan, add remaining oil and sauté onion until softened, about 5 minutes.
5. Add bell peppers and cook for 5 minutes until tender.
6. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
7. Stir in tomato paste, then add diced tomatoes, thyme, and bay leaf.
8. Season with salt and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes until tomatoes break down.
9. Return eggplant and zucchini to the pan. Gently mix everything together.
10. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 20-25 minutes, stirring occasionally.
11. Remove bay leaf. Stir in fresh basil and adjust seasoning.
12. Serve hot or at room temperature, garnished with parsley.
