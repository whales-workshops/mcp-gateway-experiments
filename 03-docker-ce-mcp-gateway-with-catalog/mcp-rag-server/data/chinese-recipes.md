# Chinese Recipes

## Classic Chinese Cuisine

### 1. [Kung Pao Chicken](kung-pao-chicken.md)
Classic Sichuan dish with tender chicken, peanuts, vegetables, and dried chilies in a spicy sauce.

### 2. [Mapo Tofu](mapo-tofu.md)
Fiery Sichuan dish with silky tofu in spicy sauce with ground pork and fermented black beans.

### 3. [Sweet and Sour Pork](sweet-and-sour-pork.md)
Crispy battered pork in tangy sauce with pineapple and bell peppers. A takeout favorite.

### 4. [Peking Duck](peking-duck.md)
Iconic Beijing dish famous for its crispy skin, served with pancakes, scallions, and hoisin sauce.

### 5. [Dan Dan Noodles](dan-dan-noodles.md)
Spicy Sichuan street food with fresh noodles in savory, nutty sauce with ground pork.
