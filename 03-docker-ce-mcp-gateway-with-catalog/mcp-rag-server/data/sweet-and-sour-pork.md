# Sweet and Sour Pork

## Summary
Sweet and Sour Pork is a beloved Chinese dish featuring crispy battered pork in a tangy, colorful sauce with pineapple and bell peppers. This crowd-pleasing recipe balances sweet and sour flavors perfectly, making it a takeout favorite you can master at home.

## Ingredients
**For the pork:**
- 1.5 lbs pork tenderloin, cut into 1-inch cubes
- 1 egg, beaten
- 1/2 cup cornstarch
- 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- Oil for deep frying

**For the sauce:**
- 1/3 cup rice vinegar
- 1/3 cup sugar
- 1/4 cup ketchup
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch
- 1/2 cup water
- 1/2 cup pineapple juice

**For the stir-fry:**
- 1 red bell pepper, cut into 1-inch pieces
- 1 green bell pepper, cut into 1-inch pieces
- 1 onion, cut into 1-inch pieces
- 1 cup pineapple chunks (fresh or canned)
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

## Preparation
1. Season pork cubes with salt and white pepper. Let stand for 10 minutes.
2. In a bowl, mix cornstarch and flour. In another bowl, beat the egg.
3. Dip pork pieces in egg, then coat thoroughly in cornstarch mixture.
4. Heat oil to 350Â°F in a deep pot or wok. Fry pork in batches until golden and crispy, about 4-5 minutes. Drain on paper towels.
5. For the sauce: Whisk together vinegar, sugar, ketchup, soy sauce, cornstarch, water, and pineapple juice in a bowl.
6. Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat.
7. Add garlic and stir-fry for 15 seconds until fragrant.
8. Add bell peppers and onion. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until slightly softened but still crisp.
9. Pour in the sauce mixture and bring to a boil. Stir constantly until sauce thickens, about 2 minutes.
10. Add pineapple chunks and stir for 30 seconds to heat through.
11. Add crispy pork and quickly toss to coat in sauce.
12. Serve immediately over steamed rice.
