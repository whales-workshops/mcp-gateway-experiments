# Dan Dan Noodles

## Summary
Dan Dan Noodles is a spicy Sichuan street food classic featuring fresh noodles in a savory, nutty sauce with ground pork and preserved vegetables. The combination of sesame paste, chili oil, and Sichuan peppercorns creates an addictively complex flavor profile.

## Ingredients
**For the noodles:**
- 1 lb fresh Chinese wheat noodles (or dried)
- 4 cups baby bok choy or Chinese greens, chopped

**For the pork topping:**
- 1/2 lb ground pork
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 2 tablespoons Sichuan preserved vegetable (zhacai), rinsed and minced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon Shaoxing wine

**For the sauce:**
- 3 tablespoons Chinese sesame paste (or tahini)
- 3 tablespoons soy sauce
- 2 tablespoons black vinegar
- 2 tablespoons chili oil (with sediment)
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 2 teaspoons Sichuan peppercorns, toasted and ground
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 cup chicken stock or noodle cooking water
- 2 tablespoons sesame oil

**For garnish:**
- 3 green onions, finely chopped
- 2 tablespoons roasted peanuts, crushed
- Fresh cilantro (optional)

## Preparation
1. For the sauce: In a bowl, whisk sesame paste with chicken stock until smooth. Add soy sauce, vinegar, chili oil, sugar, ground Sichuan peppercorns, garlic, and sesame oil. Mix well and set aside.
2. For the pork: Heat oil in a wok over high heat. Add ground pork and break up with spatula. Cook until crispy and browned, about 6 minutes.
3. Add preserved vegetable and garlic. Stir-fry for 1 minute.
4. Add soy sauce and Shaoxing wine. Cook until liquid evaporates. Remove from heat and set aside.
5. Bring a large pot of water to boil. Cook noodles according to package directions until al dente.
6. In the last minute of cooking, add bok choy to the pot.
7. Reserve 1 cup of noodle cooking water, then drain noodles and greens.
8. Divide sauce among 4 serving bowls.
9. Add hot noodles and greens to each bowl. Toss to coat in sauce. If too thick, add reserved noodle water to thin.
10. Top each bowl with ground pork mixture.
11. Garnish with green onions, crushed peanuts, and cilantro if using.
12. Serve immediately and mix well before eating.
